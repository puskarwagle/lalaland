Registrations will open at 10am EST on Wednesday 24th September 2025 until 1pm EST Wednesday 24th September 2025 (release period).  Successful participants will be contacted up by 5pm EST on Wednesday 24th September 2025 to secure your deposit appointment. If you are unsuccessful in securing an appointment, you will be notified via email and invited to participate in further releases.

During your appointment, you will be required to confirm your lot selection, provide a copy of your ID (drivers licence, passport or photo card), solicitors details, Tax File Number, bank details and make payment of the $2000 initial deposit into our legal teams trust account.
